Frequently Asked Questions

Do California and New York have different OSHA rules than federal standards?

Yes — states like California and New York operate their own OSHA-approved plans with stricter rules and reporting requirements than the federal OSHA baseline.

What should an OSHA kit for California include?

A California-specific OSHA kit should include Cal/OSHA-compliant signage, an Injury and Illness Prevention Program (IIPP), safety data sheets, and appropriate first aid supplies.

Can I use one kit for multiple states?

Only if the kit is multi-state certified. Otherwise, it's best to get kits tailored to each state’s unique OSHA plan to avoid fines or violations.
